Our Story


Dr. Eggloo, a family-owned farm with a 15-year legacy, specializes in providing pure village country chicken eggs (சிறுவிடை, பெருவிடை hens). We're committed to offering these nutrient-rich eggs to promote the healthy development of children aged 2 to 10 years old. Our promise is that 100% of our eggs come from authentic country chickens without any hybrid breeds.
